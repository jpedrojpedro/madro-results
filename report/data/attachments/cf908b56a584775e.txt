The @eusougabriela (Gábi) profile shares various lifestyle, wellness, and self-development tips. Based on her content, here are some of her key recommendations:

### **Mindset and Personal Development**
*   **Trust the Process:** She emphasizes that the secret to life is to "trust the process."
*   **Let Go of Control:** She advises that you should only take control of things you can actually influence; for everything else, she suggests you "let it go."
*   **Avoid Complaining:** She notes that complaining only makes a situation worse.
*   **Value Your Time:** She encourages followers to know the difference between waiting and losing time.
*   **Healthy Boundaries:** She suggests not making life easier for others if it comes at the expense of making your own life more difficult.
*   **Communication:** She promotes "Non-Violent Communication" as a daily exercise to express yourself clearly, calmly, and without violence to navigate social interactions effectively.
*   **Celebrate Wins:** She highlights the importance of celebrating even small victories.
*   **Self-Reflection:** She encourages practices such as the "mirror exercise" and invites followers to engage with her "Life Reset School" (@liferesetschool) for further personal growth.

### **Well-being and Nutrition**
*   **Healthy Choices:** She shares recipes for healthy treats, such as a "healthy Twix" (made with almond flour, coconut/oat flour, honey/molasses, peanut butter, and zero-sugar chocolate) and tips for a "healthy cappuccino."
*   **Skincare:** She advocates for a consistent skincare routine using her own line (@gaabwellness) and tools like facial brushes and gua shas to achieve "perfect skin in minutes."

### **Relationships and Perspective**
*   She emphasizes the value of genuine friendship and expresses deep gratitude for motherhood, noting how her children have strengthened her faith and capacity for love. She also highlights the importance of surrounding yourself with people who bring happiness and possess a real, positive spirit.
