*   **Unizushi:** Located on Floor L1 of Balneário Shopping, this restaurant specializes in Japanese cuisine.
*   **Madero**
*   **Outback**
*   **Las Lenas**
*   **Pecorino**
*   **Duladu:** An Italian restaurant also located on Floor L1 of the Balneário Shopping.
