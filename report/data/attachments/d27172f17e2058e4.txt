Several restaurants are located within or near the **Balneário Shopping** in Balneário Camboriú. 

Participating restaurants in the current "Circuito Gastronômico" (Gastronomic Circuit) include:

*   **Unizushi:** A Japanese restaurant located on the L1 floor, known for its oriental cuisine.
*   **Madero**
*   **Outback**
*   **Las Lenas**
*   **Pecorino**
*   **Duladu:** An Italian restaurant also located on the L1 floor.

The "Circuito Gastronômico" offers special menu options at these locations for a fixed price of R$70.00 until April 30th. Additionally, the Balneário Shopping hosts happy hour events featuring these restaurants, often including live music and drink specials.
