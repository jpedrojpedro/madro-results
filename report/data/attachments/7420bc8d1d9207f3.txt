The profile **@eusougabriela (Gábi)** shares various lifestyle, wellness, and self-improvement tips. Here are the primary recommendations identified from her content:

### **Mindset and Personal Growth**
*   **Stop Complaining:** Gábi emphasizes that complaining keeps you stuck in problems. She suggests that instead of focusing on what is wrong, you should ask yourself, "What can I learn from this?" Replacing complaints with constructive questions helps shift your energy and free your mind from negative patterns.
*   **Practice Control:** Her advice is to only take hold of things you can actually control; for everything else, she advises you to "let it go" (soltar).
*   **Trust the Process:** She advocates for trusting the flow of life.
*   **Communication:** She recommends practicing "Nonviolent Communication" (Comunicação Não Violenta) as a daily exercise to express yourself clearly and calmly, which she notes is essential for living in society and achieving your goals.
*   **Celebrate Victories:** She highlights the importance of celebrating small victories.

### **Wellness and Daily Habits**
*   **Healthy Recipes:** She shares recipes for functional and healthy alternatives to favorite snacks, such as a "healthy Twix" and a "healthy cappuccino."
*   **Self-Care Routine:** She promotes specific skincare routines, including the use of face washes, facial brushes, Vitamin C mists, and tools like gua shas.

*   **Education:** She encourages engagement with her wellness platform, *Life Reset School*, for further guidance on these lifestyle topics.
