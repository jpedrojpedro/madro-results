Based on the content shared by Gábi (@eusougabriela), her lifestyle tips focus on personal development, emotional regulation, and wellness habits. Here are some of her key recommendations:

### **Mindset and Personal Growth**
*   **Trust the Process:** She emphasizes that the secret to life is to "trust the process."
*   **Practice Non-Violent Communication:** She suggests reading books on non-violent communication to express yourself clearly and calmly, noting that this is a fundamental daily exercise for better social interaction.
*   **Maintain Control:** Her advice is to only engage with or try to control what is within your power; for everything else, she advises you to "let it go."
*   **Avoid Complaining:** She highlights that the more you complain, the worse a situation becomes.
*   **Discernment:** She encourages followers to know the difference between waiting for something and simply losing time, and suggests prioritizing your own well-being by not facilitating others' lives if it comes at the expense of your own.
*   **Celebrate Small Wins:** She advocates for the importance of recognizing and celebrating small victories.

### **Wellness and Habits**
*   **Skincare Routine:** She shares specific routines using products from her brand, @gaabwellness, and tools like facial brushes and guachás to achieve a "peach-like" skin.
*   **Healthy Eating:** She provides healthy recipes, such as a "healthy Twix" made with almond flour, coconut flour, honey, and zero-sugar chocolate, as well as tips for making healthy beverages like a healthy cappuccino.

### **Community and Connection**
*   **Life Reset School:** She frequently directs followers to the @liferesetschool for further guidance on these lifestyle and wellness topics.
*   **Self-Reflection:** She encourages practices such as the "mirror exercise" to facilitate personal growth.

*Note: While Gábi shares many wellness tips, she also emphasizes that for her, life is centered around family, faith, and the capacity for love.*
