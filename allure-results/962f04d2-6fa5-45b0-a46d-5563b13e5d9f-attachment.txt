### **Mindset and Personal Growth**
*   **Practice Non-Violent Communication:** She suggests reading books on non-violent communication, noting that learning to express yourself with clarity and calm is a fundamental exercise for living well in society.
*   **Master Control:** Her philosophy is to only engage with what you can control and to "let go" of everything else.
*   **Trust the Process:** She emphasizes that the secret to life is trusting the process.
*   **Avoid Excessive Complaining:** She notes that the more you complain, the worse your situation becomes.
*   **Value Your Time:** She encourages followers to know the difference between "waiting" and "losing time" and suggests prioritizing your own well-being rather than making life easier for others at the expense of your own.
*   **Celebrate Small Wins:** She highlights the importance of recognizing and celebrating even the smallest personal achievements.

### **Wellness and Daily Habits**
